Faithfulness measures whether generated answers are supported by retrieved documents. High faithfulness means answers are grounded in evidence.

BM25 is a lexical retrieval algorithm. It relies on term frequency. It does not understand semantic meaning.

Dense retrieval uses embeddings. It captures semantic similarity between queries and documents.

Cross-encoders rerank retrieved documents. They are slower but more accurate than bi-encoders.

Query expansion generates multiple variations of a query. It improves recall in retrieval systems.

Query rewriting removes ambiguity from user queries. It improves retrieval precision.

Compression reduces token usage. It selects only relevant parts of documents.
